About

I've spent my career figuring out how to get people to genuinely engage with hard ideas. Not just present them — engage with them. That requires understanding how people think, what makes them feel safe enough to participate, and what it takes to hold space for the complicated thing instead of collapsing it into something easier.

That instinct runs through everything I do: the research, the foresight methodology, the games, the writing. The medium changes. The project is the same.

Based in Los Angeles. Originally from Japan.
